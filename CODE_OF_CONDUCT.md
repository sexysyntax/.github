# Code of Conduct

SexySyntax is part of the SHNWAZ DEV developer ecosystem. We want the community to be useful, welcoming, and safe for people who are building together.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Help people understand the work.
- Give feedback about code, docs, ideas, and behavior, not personal attacks.
- Credit other people when using their work or suggestions.
- Keep discussions focused and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Spam, scams, unrelated promotion, or repeated duplicate posts.
- Sharing private credentials, tokens, personal data, or sensitive information.
- Deliberately misleading maintainers or contributors.
- Disrupting issues, pull requests, or discussions after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, move, lock, or delete content that breaks these rules. Maintainers may also limit participation for users who repeatedly ignore the community guidelines.

If you see a problem, contact an organization maintainer at https://github.com/shnwazdeveloper.
